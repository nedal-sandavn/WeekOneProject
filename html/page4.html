The website that I will share with you is considered

Life saver

For those who want to practice writing and cannot create an idea from scratch
You must have heard of people who could write an entire story because of one word or sentence
This is what this site helps you do

  • Random Character Traits Generator:

     This tool randomly chooses three personality traits for your character.

  • Quick Plot Generator:

    This writing tool generates a random idea for a story at the click of a button.

  • Random First Line Prompts:

     The aim of these writing prompts is to spark off a short story.
    When you click the button, a random first line for a story will be generated.

  • Random First Line of Dialogue:

     The aim of these writing prompts is to help with dialogue writing.
    This can be useful for scriptwriting and screenwriting, as well as narrative writing.
    a random first line of dialogue will be generated. Your task is to continue the conversation.

  • Random Plot Generator:

     The aim of this writing prompt is to help you develop a story-line. When you click the buttons,
    they will generate two characters, a setting, a situation and a theme.
    Your job is to put the elements together and come up with an idea for a story.
